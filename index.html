Discovery Questionnaire

Complete guide for conducting effective customer discovery calls

1 Pre-Meeting Preparation

Research Checklist

Materials to Prepare

SECTION 1 20 MINUTES

Current Process Discovery

"Can you walk me through your [specific process] from start to finish? Pretend I'm a new employee on your team learning this for the first time."

Trigger & Frequency

  • - "What triggers this process to start?"
  • - "How often does this happen per day/week/month?"
  • - "Are there specific times or patterns?"

Steps & Systems

  • - "Then what happens next?"
  • - "What system do you go into for that?"
  • - "What information do you need at this step?"

People & Decisions

  • - "Who is involved at each step?"
  • - "Who makes decisions here?"
  • - "How do they get that information?"

Exceptions & Escalations

  • - "What happens when the normal process doesn't work?"
  • - "When do you escalate? To whom?"
  • - "Can you give me a recent example?"
SECTION 2 15 MINUTES

Business Impact Quantification

"This sounds like a significant effort. Help me understand the business impact when things go wrong or take too long."

Direct Costs

  • - "What does it cost when [problem] happens?"
  • - "How much are you paying in detention/penalties?"
  • - "Can you share rough numbers, even ballpark?"

Labor Costs

  • - "How many people work on this process?"
  • - "How many hours per day/week?"
  • - "What strategic work isn't getting done?"

Service & Compliance

  • - "How does this affect customer relationships?"
  • - "What's your current performance vs target?"
  • - "Are there contractual SLAs you need to meet?"

Risk & Reputation

  • - "What's at risk if this continues?"
  • - "Have you lost customers over this?"
  • - "What would happen if this got worse?"
SECTION 3 15 MINUTES

Desired Future State

"Now let's talk about the ideal state. If we could wave a magic wand and fix all of this, what would it look like?"

Automation Desires

  • - "What parts should happen automatically?"
  • - "Where do you still want human involvement?"
  • - "What decisions should remain manual?"

Visibility Needs

  • - "What information do you wish you had?"
  • - "What would you want to know proactively?"
  • - "How would you want to be alerted?"

Success Definition

  • - "How would you measure success?"
  • - "What KPIs matter most to leadership?"
  • - "What would 'great' look like in 6 months?"
SECTION 4 10 MINUTES

Constraints & Considerations

"Before we design your solution, what constraints or requirements should we keep in mind?"

Technical

  • - "What systems must we integrate with?"
  • - "Security or compliance requirements?"
  • - "Any systems we cannot touch?"

Organizational

  • - "Who needs to approve this project?"
  • - "Any change management concerns?"
  • - "Previous automation experiences?"

Scope & Timeline

  • - "Start with specific locations/carriers?"
  • - "What's explicitly out of scope?"
  • - "Any critical dates we should know?"

What to Listen For

Manual steps that could be automated
Waiting/delays between steps
Multiple system logins
Excel spreadsheets
Email/phone communications
Repeated "checking" or "monitoring"
Frustration words: "always", "never"
Time spent on repetitive tasks
